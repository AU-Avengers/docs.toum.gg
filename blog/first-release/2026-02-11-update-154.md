## Town of Us: Mira has updated to [v1.5.4](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira/releases/tag/v1.5.4)!

This update includes bug fixes and improved map configurations! Be sure to do a fresh install.

See changelog below for bugfixes, changes and improvements.

<!--truncate-->

### Changelog

<details className="customdetails">
  <summary>**Role Bugfixes**</summary>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Crewmate Roles**</summary>
* Mirrorcaster no longer bypasses their cooldowns.
</details>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Neutral Roles**</summary>
* Soul Collected bodies are now removed upon revival.
</details></details>

<details className="customdetails">
  <summary>**Miscellaneous Bugfixes**</summary>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Modifier Fixes**</summary>
* Deadly Quota's guess setting is checked correctly again.
* Taskmaster no longer activates when the player is a Vampire/Traitor.
</details>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Other Bug Fixes**</summary>
* Deaths should no longer desync under race conditions with meetings.
* Kills can no longer be triggered in the lobby from cheat clients.
* Role icons will no longer appear behind text in the role tab.
</details>
</details>

<details className="customdetails">
  <summary>**Additions and Known Issues**</summary>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Additions**</summary>
* Visual Theme toggles for Skeld, Mira, and Polus can now be adjusted.
* Add Required Mods setting for hosts.
</details>

<details className="customdetails">
<summary>**Known Issues**</summary>
* Sentry's portable cameras are completely broken on Starlight.
</details>
</details>
