## [Town of Us: Reactivated is so back!](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/Town-Of-Us-Reactivated)

Our very own [Atony](https://github.com/AtonyGit) has officially updated **Town of Us: Reactivated** to run on the latest version of Among Us!

Going forward, updates for TOU:R will be for compatibility and quality of life. New roles and modifiers will **not** be added to this release. See the changelog below for more.
